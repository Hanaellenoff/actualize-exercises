Recipe for Muffins

make sure to have two bowls to put the first half of the ingrediants into

and a second one to put the second half into

The first bowl

  1. 1/2 cup carrots
  2. 2 cups brown sugar
  3. as much oil as u want >D

The second bowl

  1. 5 eggs
  2. 2 cups sugar
  3. 3 cups flour

How to make

  • Make sure to stir eggs Link to beating eggs properly
  • cut up your carrots
  • put the two bowls together after mixing both well
  • put oven to 350 and and put in muffin pan with cupcake holders
